The goal is to create an app that blends global design trends like Korean minimalism and Italian luxury with Indian architectural sensibilities, solving key user pain points through technological innovation.

User Insights

Understanding the target user is paramount. This section details the primary frustrations Indian homeowners face during the interior design process and outlines specific UX/UI design optimizations required to build an app that is not just functional, but truly intuitive and trustworthy for the Indian audience.

Key User Pain Points

  • ✘
    Visualization Gap: Users struggle to imagine how 2D mood boards or furniture will look in their actual 3D space.
  • ✘
    Choice Paralysis: Infinite options on platforms like Pinterest lead to confusion and indecision.
  • ✘
    Budget Uncertainty: Fear of hidden costs and difficulty in tracking expenses against the initial budget.
  • ✘
    Finding Authenticity: Difficulty in finding unique, non-generic items and connecting with local artisans.

UX Optimizations for India

  • ✔
    Visual & Vernacular: Prioritize high-quality visuals and offer multilingual support (Hindi, regional languages).
  • ✔
    Low-Data & Offline First: Optimize image loading and provide offline access to saved designs for areas with poor connectivity.
  • ✔
    Trust & Transparency: Integrate WhatsApp for quick communication. Display clear pricing with EMI options. Feature user reviews prominently.
  • ✔
    Cultural Nuance: Include features that resonate culturally, such as a Vastu-compliance checker, and showcase traditional Indian crafts.

The Vision: Innovative Features

To differentiate from the competition and directly address user pain points, AuraSpaces will be built around a core set of innovative, technology-driven features. These functionalities aim to make the design process personalized, visual, and seamless. Click on each feature below to learn more about how it contributes to the unique value proposition of the app.

1. AI Style Blender

▾

Users select multiple design influences (e.g., 'Italian Luxury', 'Korean Minimalism', 'Jaipuri Craft'). The AI engine generates unique mood boards, color palettes, and product recommendations that fuse these styles, overcoming choice paralysis with guided, personalized inspiration.

2. Live AR Space Planner

▾

This feature directly solves the visualization gap. Using their phone's camera, users can place true-to-scale 3D models of furniture and decor items into their actual room, viewable from any angle. This provides an unparalleled understanding of size, fit, and style in context.

3. Vastu Score & Advisor

▾

Tapping into cultural values, users can upload their floor plan, and the app's AI will provide a Vastu-compliance score. It will highlight areas of concern and suggest practical, design-friendly remedies for element placement, colors, and directions, adding a layer of trust and relevance.

4. Curated Artisan Marketplace

▾

To combat generic designs and promote local craftsmanship, this feature provides a dedicated marketplace. Users can discover and purchase unique items—like Blue Pottery, Dhokra art, or hand-woven textiles—directly from vetted Indian artisans, adding an authentic touch to their homes.

Design Blueprint for Figma Prototype

This final section translates all preceding analysis into a concrete set of actionable insights for the UI/UX design phase. These principles should guide the creation of the Figma prototype, ensuring the final design is user-centric, strategically sound, and poised for success in the Indian market.

Core User Journey

The primary flow must be a guided three-step process:

  1. Inspire Me: Onboarding with the AI Style Blender.
  2. Visualize It: Seamless transition to the AR Space Planner.
  3. Make It Mine: Easy access to product pages and the Artisan Marketplace.

Home Screen "Hero"

The landing screen should not be a generic gallery. It must prominently feature the "AI Style Blender" as the primary call-to-action. This immediately communicates the app's unique value proposition.

Build Trust Visually

Incorporate trust signals throughout the app. This includes featuring designer profiles with user ratings, "AuraSpaces Assured" badges on products, transparent cost calculators, and authentic customer testimonials with photos.

Seamless Interactions

Design for one-tap actions. Access to the AR viewer from a product page should be instant. Sharing designs on WhatsApp should be a native function. The interface must feel fluid, modern, and responsive.
